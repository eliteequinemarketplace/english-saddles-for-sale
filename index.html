Antares saddles are prized for their quality French leather and close-contact jumping models. Voltaire Design saddles are among the most recognized premium jumping saddles in the sport. Both brands hold strong resale value and are consistently sought after on the used saddle market.

Buying a Used Saddle — What to Know

Always have a saddle fitter evaluate a used saddle for your horse's back before finalizing a purchase. Check leather and tree condition, size suitability, and the seller's return or trial policy.
